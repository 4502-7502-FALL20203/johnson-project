Korean-Pop music originated in South Korea. Key characteristics include idol-groups, fan-culture, high-quality production, variety shows, and an extremely strong international appeal.

10. Love Scenario by IKON

The song's lyrics describe the stages of a love story, from the excitement of the beginning to the pain of the breakup, and ultimately to the acceptance and nostalgia that follow. It conveys a sense of longing and sentimentality that resonated with many listeners.

9. 10 Minutes by Lee Hyori

In the song, Lee Hyori sings about wanting to spend just 10 minutes with someone she has feelings for, emphasizing the intensity of her emotions in that short span of time.

8. LO$ER=LO♡ER by TXT

LO$ER=LO♡ER" is a pop/rock track with a catchy and energetic melody. The lyrics of the song explore themes of love and loss, and they convey a sense of yearning and longing for a past relationship.

7. Digital Lover by Crush

“Digital Lover” is about how we have become introverted in the sense that we have become too wary of everyone else.

6. Paradise by BTS

"Paradise" is a track that offers encouragement and motivation to its listeners. In the song, BTS acknowledges the pressures and hardships people face in their pursuit of dreams and success, and they offer reassurance that it's okay to take a break and not be too hard on oneself.

5. Text Me by DPR Live

"Text Me" is a track where DPR Live discusses the struggles and emotions that can come with modern communication, particularly in the context of relationships. The song explores themes related to digital communication, such as text messages, and the disconnect that can occur despite being connected virtually.

4. Merlot by Nafla

"Merlot" is known for its mellow and laid-back vibe, with Nafla delivering his verses in a smooth and rhythmic manner. The lyrics may vary in meaning and interpretation, as is common in hip-hop music, but they often touch on personal experiences, emotions, or storytelling.

3. What is Love by EXO

In "What is Love," EXO sings about the uncertainty and complexity of love. The lyrics express feelings of confusion and curiosity about what love truly means, whether it's romantic love, familial love, or other forms of affection. The song's melody is catchy, and it features the group's signature harmonies and vocal performances.

2. Mmmh by Kai

"Mmmh" is a pop/R&B track that showcases Kai's smooth vocals and performance abilities. The song has a sensual and contemporary R&B sound, with lyrics that convey themes of attraction and desire.

1. Fake Love by BTS

The song is a blend of pop and hip-hop and addresses the theme of a love that has turned out to be deceptive and insincere.
