My web pages depict different aspects of my life. They include some of my favorite restaurants I encountered during my time in Seattle this summer, song recommendations from my favorite music genre, and behind-the-scenes glimpses of my dance team practice—where I spent most of my final semester in college. For the theme of my pages, the colors I chose were intentional. After graduation, I'm moving to Seattle, also known as the Emerald City. The colors I chose are the colors of my favorite Seattle sports team—the Mariners! As a future Seattleite, I wanted to incorporate the city throughout my website pages. Instead of telling a story, I'm more so giving the viewer a glimpse into my life, into my personality—things I love, and a bit of my day-to-day. I hope that in my pages, what comes across is my joy for the simple things in life, such as a good meal, enjoyable music, and loving company. As I continue on my journey to create platforms that showcase who I am, I want the same energy to be reciprocated across every page I make. I wanted them to be concise and easy on the eye, and I think I accomplished it well. Enjoy getting to know a little bit about me!
